\begin{exercice}\label{exoOutilsMath-0063}

    Calculer la circulation de
    \begin{equation}
        F(x,y,z)=\begin{pmatrix}
            \cos(z)    \\ 
            e^x    \\ 
            e^y    
        \end{pmatrix}
    \end{equation}
    le long du chemin $\sigma\colon \mathopen[ 0 , 2 \mathclose]\to \begin{pmatrix}
        1    \\ 
        t    \\ 
        e^{t}    
    \end{pmatrix}$.
