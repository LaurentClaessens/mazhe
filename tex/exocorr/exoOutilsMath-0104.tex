\begin{exercice}\label{exoOutilsMath-0104}

    On pose $D=\{ (x,y)\in\eR^2\tq 0\leq x\leq 1,0\leq y\leq 1,x^2+y^2\geq1 \}$. Calculer
    \begin{equation}
        \iint_{D}\frac{ xy }{ 1+x^2+y^2 }dxdy.
    \end{equation}
